Dear King Grol

 I trust this message reaches you amidst your affairs, and I hope that your health remains unblemished. I must confess that I write to you today with a heavy heart and no small amount of consternation.

 It has come to my attention that you have, in my view, relinquished control over Gundren Rockseeker and the map of Wave Echo Cave to individuals whom I consider beneath both our stations. This turn of events has left me deeply dismayed. This grievous oversight has ensnared my sensibilities in a profound state of disquietude. I had, it appears, entrusted you with a weighty responsibility: to guard these sacrosanct assets and bestow them upon the deserving.

 However, I am not without patience, King Grol. I do concede, however, that life's vicissitudes can render even the most stalwart of leaders vulnerable to error. Therefore, I extend an opportunity for us to amend this situation.

 In acknowledgment of your esteemed leadership and your adherence to principles befitting one of our stature, I extend an opportunity to recalibrate our endeavors with a new arrangement: I am willing to compensate you generously in exchange for the services of your formidable goblinoid warriors and assassins and the safe return of the infant you have collected recently.

 My network of spies, ever vigilant, has observed your decisive actions in dealing with the adventurers' abandoned wagon near Venomfang's lair. Venomfang, despite his occasional capriciousness, poses no substantial threat to your dominion.
